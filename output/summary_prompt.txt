内容: Over the past few months it's become clear that coding agents are extraordinarily good at building a weird version of a "clean room" implementation of code. The most famous version of this pattern is when Compaq created a clean-room clone of the IBM BIOS back in 1982 . They had one team of engineers reverse engineer the BIOS to create a specification, then handed that specification to another team to build a new ground-up version.
